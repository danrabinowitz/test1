GDPR Privacy Notice For All 2019 Invitees:
Due to the 2018 implementation of the GDPR data protection and privacy law, all approved nominees will be processed in the Global E-mail Management Service (“GEMS”) - an email cleansing database which provides the subscription status indicating whether your customer has opted out of unsolicited emails from Cisco, including the invitation for Executive Symposium.

If the approved customer is on the opt-out suppression list, AMs will be the only contact notified via email from Jenny Damitio (jdamitio@cisco.com), providing a copy of the personalized invite to forward on to the customer. You will also see your customer’s profile highlighted in red within the CECC database.

For more information regarding GDPR, visit our page here.
